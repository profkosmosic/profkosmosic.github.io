WHAT I DO

WEB DESIGN AND PROGRAMMING

Through ICT College I have acquired a much better understanding and a firmer grasp of programming as a concept and I have went on to write programs and develop websites of my own very shortly after. As of now I have made one full website using HTML and CSS, written many lines of code in both C and C# and built several games on my own, one of which so far has been released publicly.

GAME DEVELOPMENT

Game development has been a hobby of mine since I was in primary school and is the main reason why today I have an interest in programming. I mostly use Unity and Game Maker Studio when I want to make my own games from scratch, but sometimes I also modify already existing games such as Half-Life 2 (Source Engine) by making new levels or editing/replacing the game's textures and such.

VIDEO EDITING

I have been making and editing videos as assignments since the beginning of high school. I have experience using both Sony Vegas and Adobe Premiere for editing(green screen, syncing etc.) and Adobe After Effects for all kinds of VFX. Though many of my works have either been deleted to save space or lost over the years, I have kept the most recent video that I have edited together for my finals.
